Fiber Laser Cutting Machine

热销产品

产品描述

Fiber Laser Welding Machine is a kind of high energy density laser beam as a heat source, locally heating the material in a small range, so that the material is melted to form a specific melt pool, so as to achieve the welding effect. Laser welding is mainly used in the welding of thin-wall materials and precision parts. It can realize spot welding, splicing welding, sealing welding, lap welding, etc., small thermal deformation, beautiful and smooth weld, high quality weld, no need for secondary grinding treatment. In recent years, laser welding has been widely used in stainless steel, carbon steel, galvanized sheet, copper, aluminum and other products welding. The hand-held laser welding machine uses the hand-held swing welding head, the operation is flexible and simple, does not require professional welder skills, and the welding efficiency is high. It can perfectly replace traditional argon arc welding, electric welding and other processes, and is suitable for small batch and mass production and processing.
